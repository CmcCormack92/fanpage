The Menzingers Fan Page

Bio

The Menzingers are an American Punk/indie rock band out of Scranton, Pennsylvania. Since the band formed in 2006, they have gone on to release seven studio albums. Their lineup consists of Tom May and Greg Barnett both on guitar and vocals, Eric Keen on bass and Joe Godino on the drums. To read more about The Menzingers visit their official site.

Greatest hits include:

  • "Rodent"
  • "The Obituaries"
  • "Good Things"
  • "Gates"
  • "After the Party"
  • "Anna"
  • "Strangers Forever"
  • "Lookers"
  • "The Talk"
  • "Ave Maria"
